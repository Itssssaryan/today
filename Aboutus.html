About Us

Code IT is a software development training center that offers a range of courses and programs for individuals interested in learning about software development. Located in the heart of the city (Dharan), Code IT is easily accessible and boasts state-of-the-art facilities and resources for students.

The team at Code IT is composed of experienced professionals in the field of software development, who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and expertise with the next generation of programmers. We offer a variety of courses, ranging from beginner-level introductions to programming to more advanced topics for experienced developers.

Some of the courses offered at Code IT include:

Introduction to Programming: This course is designed for those with little or no programming experience. It covers the basics of programming languages such as Python and Java, and introduces students to concepts such as data types, variables, and control structures.

Web Development: This course teaches students how to build and design websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Students will learn how to create responsive, user-friendly web pages and will have the opportunity to work on projects to hone their skills.

Mobile App Development: In this course, students will learn how to develop native mobile apps for Android and iOS platforms. They will learn how to design and implement user interfaces, connect to APIs, and publish their apps to the App Store or Google Play.

In addition to these courses, Code IT also offers a range of workshops and events for students to learn about specific technologies and techniques, and to network with other professionals in the field.

Overall, Code IT is a top-quality software development training center that is dedicated to helping students develop the skills they need to succeed in the competitive world of software development.
